About The Site

This site was created totally inspired by the series created by Netflix "how to sell drugs online (fast)".

In order to win back his ex-girlfriend, who is in a relationship with the school's drug dealer, Moritz takes a bold step: selling better drugs over the internet. With the help of Lenny, his best friend, he creates an online narcotics market, and in a short time, they become one of the biggest drug dealers in Europe.
